Want to know me better?

I’m a very enthusiastic person who loves to learn from everything that surrounds me. I believe in the idea that every new day can bring us new experiences and I’m always willing to do/try new things.

Do you want to know what I do? I’m a UX designer (If you’re here, you probably already knew hehe). I’ve always had an innate capability to understand people’s issues and look for a solution that suits them best. So that’s what I do when thinking and developing a design. I try to understand what is asked, needed, left… I put on user’s shoes and then walk on them to approach for the best solution through my design.

As someone who has a background on videogame industry, I’ve developed some skills which are very useful nowadays in this career. I have an open mind and I’m already used to work next to developers and artist and put them in common, always with the initial idea of what we are creating and what is demanded.
